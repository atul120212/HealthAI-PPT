Context & Problem

The Public Healthcare Bottleneck

Rural Primary Health Centres (PHCs) in India serve massive populations but suffer from key operational friction points that impede delivery.

ASHA Worker Overburden

ASHA workers spend 40% of their working hours on administrative coordination and manually checking beneficiary eligibility details.

Navigation Friction

First-time patients waste critical time navigating complex PHC hallways, pharmacies, and registration counters due to lack of signage.

Language Disconnect

Official health materials and scheme portals are often not accessible in local dialects (Kannada, Tamil, rural Hindi), causing information drop-off.

aarogya.citizen.health/
Module 1: Aarogya Voice Portal

Citizen Voice Assistant

A simplified, voice-first screen built for citizens. Features a central pulsing orb indicating real-time voice state (VAD).

  • Natural Slot Filling Autonomously collects appointment reason, date, time (resolves relative terms like "tomorrow"), patient name, and email.
  • Seamless Identity Verification Extracts phone numbers or ABHA IDs via natural voice input and queries Supabase to personalise responses.
  • Ayushman Bharat Pre-Eligibility Check Instantly informs citizens of their scheme status and coordinates active hospital departments instructions.
sahayak.healthworker.health/
Module 2: Sahayak Clinical AI

ASHA & Nurse Copilot

An interactive dashboard built for frontline healthcare staff. Translates spoken notes into structured records.

  • ASHA Protocol Lookup Instant voice queries search RAG knowledge base for HBNC visit schedules, ORS dosages, and vaccination rules.
  • Voice Record updates ASHA workers describe patient symptoms naturally (e.g. "Rama Devi, BP 130 over 85") and the system extracts structured fields in real time.
  • Referral Guidance Banner Visually flags "Emergency" or "Urgent" referrals, rendering flashing visual alerts and structured action points.
surveillance.district.health/
Module 3: Surveillance & Analytics

Early Outbreak Detection

A reporting dashboard for District Medical Officers. Aggregates voice interaction logs into statistical outbreak indicators.

  • Automated Outbreak Alerts Monitors patient transcripts for disease keywords (e.g. Diarrhea, Dengue). Triggers alerts based on Z-score spikes.
  • Interactive Outbreak Hotspot Map Renders district-level geographical risks visually, color-coded from low (green) to critical (red).
  • 8-Week Sparkline Trends Provides instant scannable trend charts for major infectious diseases to aid preventive dispatch.
